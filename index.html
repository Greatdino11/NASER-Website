NASER

Neuroscience Association of Service, Education, and Research
A 501(c)(3) Nonprofit built by students to educate students about Neuroscience.

We teach science to students
to empower the next generation
of doctors and surgeons

Service

We provide our students with many service opportunities with local hospitals and clinics, helping them achieve real-world experience.

Education

We provide education to our students structured to set them for success going into the medical field. Our curriculum is designed by us students for the next generation of medical students.

Research

We provide high quality research opportunities for our students. They tackle real-world problems and create ground-breaking and innovative solutions to them.
